## Overview

Build an original direct-to-consumer telehealth platform inspired by Ro: condition-focused care paths (weight management, sexual health, hair health), clinician-led ongoing care, labs when required, prescription fulfillment, and subscription refills.

This spec is Draft 1: surfaces ready; state licensure orchestration, weight-loss medication eligibility, controlled-substance policy, and lab integrations remain behind compliance/clinical review.

## Goals

- Care-path intake per condition with clinical protocol.
- Clinician consult (async or video) and decision.
- Lab orders and results integration when protocols require.
- Prescription fulfillment via partner pharmacy with subscription refills.
- Ongoing care messaging with clinician.
- Transparent pricing and cancel-anytime.

## Non-Goals

- Do not provide medical advice outside a clinician.
- Do not copy Ro trademarks or marketing copy.
- Do not prescribe controlled substances without compliance review.
- Do not collect PHI beyond protocol needs.

## Detailed Design

### Source-Backed Product Requirements

- Condition-focused care paths with protocol-driven intake.
- Clinician consult (async messaging; video when protocol requires).
- Lab orders with partner labs; results ingestion.
- Prescription fulfillment and subscription refills with partner pharmacy.
- Continuous check-ins and progress logging.
- Shipment tracking, pause/cancel flows.
- Clear "not for emergencies" messaging.

## Core User Journeys

- User selects a care path, completes protocol-based intake.
- Clinician reviews; requests labs or issues decision.
- User completes labs at partner location; results auto-attached.
- Clinician issues Rx and plan; user subscribes to refills.
- User logs progress (for example, weight, photos) per cadence.
- User messages clinician within scope for dose adjustments.
- User pauses or cancels subscription.
- User updates address; shipping recalculated.
- User downloads lab and visit summaries.
- User deletes account and PHI.

## Data Model

- `User`: account, state, privacy, age flags.
- `CarePath`: id, category, protocol version.
- `IntakeResponse`: care-path id, answers, schema version.
- `ClinicianReview`: intake id, clinician id, decision, notes.
- `LabOrder`: lab partner id, tests, status.
- `LabResult`: values, ranges, attachments.
- `Prescription`: drug, dose, cadence, status.
- `Subscription` / `Shipment` / `Order`: fulfillment lifecycle.
- `ProgressEntry`: type, data, timestamps.
- `AuditEvent`: PHI access and consent events.

## Realtime, Push, And Offline Behavior

- Push covers intake status, clinician messages, labs ready, Rx fulfillment, shipments.
- Push payloads exclude lab values and drug details.
- Messaging near-real-time via WebSocket or push fallback.
- Offline: intake drafts, read-only results snapshots, shipment cache.
- Crisis resources cached for mental-health-adjacent flows where applicable.

## Permissions, Privacy, And Safety

- Camera and photo library only where intake or progress requires.
- Mic and camera only at video consult.
- Notifications at contextual points.
- "Not for emergencies" banners.
- PHI minimization; encrypted-at-rest; BAAs with clinical, pharmacy, and lab partners.
- No PHI in third-party analytics or ad SDKs.
- HIPAA-compliant posture with audit logging of PHI access.
- State-licensure routing per care path.
- Weight-loss medication eligibility rules (BMI, comorbidities) enforced by clinician.
- Controlled-substance flows gated behind compliance flag.
- Minor gating per care-path rules.
- Launch owners: clinical director, privacy/HIPAA counsel, compliance, security, fulfillment ops.

## Analytics And Monetization

- Privacy-safe events: care-path entry class, approval class, subscription lifecycle.
- Monetization via visit fees and subscription.
- No PHI-linked telemetry; ad SDKs excluded from clinical surfaces.

## Edge Cases

- Intake declined; refund/flow guidance.
- Lab result out of range; clinician follow-up required.
- Weight-loss medication eligibility not met; alternative plan.
- Controlled-substance request; compliance path.
- State unsupported for care path; block and guide.
- Shipment delayed causing dose gap; clinician notification.
- Minor user in adult-only care path.
- Progress photo privacy; explicit consent.
- Partner outage; queue and backoff.
